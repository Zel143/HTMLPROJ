About INK FIST STUDIOS

INK FIST is a high-energy art studio specializing in the creation of intense fight scenes and dynamic motion. Our expertise lies in crafting fluid, powerful, and visually striking animations that bring action sequences to life with unmatched energy.

We work across multiple industries, delivering top-tier animation for video games, animated series, commercials, and blockbuster films. Whether it’s hand-drawn frame-by-frame combat or fast-paced digital choreography, we push every movement to its absolute limit.

What sets INK FIST apart is our signature style—bold, exaggerated, and unapologetically intense. We embrace sharp, expressive motion, explosive impacts, and a raw, visceral feel that demands attention. Our animations don’t just move; they hit hard.

When you need action that feels alive, INK FIST is the studio that delivers.

Our Work

INK FIST is also renowned for its masterful use of diverse coloring and shading techniques, adding depth, mood, and intensity to every scene.

A single production can showcase a variety of shading textures, color palettes, and intricate patterns, shifting seamlessly to match the tone, atmosphere, and emotion of a scene.
